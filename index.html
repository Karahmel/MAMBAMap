Hey Guys!

  • We know it is overwhelming to move to a new city, leave your job, leave your friends, and start the program all at once, so we wanted to help as much as possible with the housing search. Baltimore is a city of neighborhoods; depending on your personal needs and preferences, your ideal home could be almost anywhere- from quirky Hampden to happening Fells Point to somewhere in DC! We hope these MAMBA-made maps will help you figure out which neighborhood is most YOU so that you can focus your housing search and move in with less stress

    If you have any questions about specific neighborhoods or properties, email Karah at ksalaets@mica.edu and she can connect you with the MAMBA who lived there. You can also email with general questions about the area or with suggestions for what else to include on these maps.

    Cheers to a smooth transition and another great group of MAMBAs!
